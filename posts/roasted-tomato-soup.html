Roasted tomato soup

Roasting concentrates the tomatoes; a good blender does the rest.

Halve ripe tomatoes, add garlic cloves in their skins, a glug of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast until collapsed and jammy, then squeeze the garlic out and blend with stock to your preferred thickness.
